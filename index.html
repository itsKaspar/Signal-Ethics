Signal Ethics

Signal Ethics is an independant publication repository of critical new media art studies. Taking the form of a blog compiling articles, bits of code and net art, it serves as a permanent net-residency logbook for the work and research of Kaspar Ravel.

Under the cover of documenting their artistic practice, this website aims to offer techno-critical POVs towards new media by questioning the thought school of rationality && the neoliberal logics that dominate our networked spaces today.

The different DIY approaches presented here in the form of hypertext and code are to be seen as poelitic templates to access new and alternative forms of digital litteracy.
